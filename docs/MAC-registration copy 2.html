The registration fee includes attendance, coffee breaks, lunches, conference kit, electronic proceedings, banquet dinner on October 10 (for SSS registrants), and social dinner on October 12 (for MAC registrants).

Each accepted paper in SSS needs at least one SSS Author registration, even for student co-authored paper.

A student co-author of a SSS accepted paper can register in student category if there is already a SSS Author registration to that paper.

How to register?

Step 1: Fill the google registration form: SSS and/or MAC 2025 Registration

Step 2: Make a credit card payment selecting your registration type: Registration Payment

Step 3: (Optional) If you plan to stay in the conference hotel (Aloft Kathmandu Thamel), please fill this form: Conference Hotel Aloft
